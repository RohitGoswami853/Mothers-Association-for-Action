Our Journey so far

What is our Goal?

Mothers’ Association for Action came into being in 2017 with its mission for child education and has since been working with the objective of empowering underprivileged and adults by providing proper education. The Mission of Education programme caters to focus on children and adults. The targets are underprivileged children and adults living in difficult circumstances, children forced to work as labourers, children of the poorest of the poor parents, abandoned and street children and adults, disaster-struck children and adults.


What is our Vision?

To envision an efficient organization where, our standards are completely aligned with our goal and ethics.

Commitment : To have engaged people who have ethics with our organizational goals.
Integrity : Consistency and Predictability. To do what we are meant to do with the right attitude and commitment in order to achieve our goals.
Transparency : Confidence to deal with disreputable situation and to disclose unethical situation where standards are inconsistent with our values.
